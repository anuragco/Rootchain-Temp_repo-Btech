Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Rootchain?

RootChain is an online platform that connects farmers directly with buyers, ensuring fair prices and a stable market. It also serves as a learning hub, providing insights into modern farming techniques and efficient farm management.

Is Rootchain free to use?

Yes, RootChain is free to use for farmers and buyers.
The platform offers free access to its marketplace for direct transactions and educational resources.

What features does Rootchain offer?

RootChain offers direct farmer-to-buyer selling, fair pricing, and market stability. It serves as an educational hub for modern farming techniques, seed varieties, and farm management. The platform ensures secure transactions, provides smart farm management tools, and fosters a community for knowledge sharing, empowering farmers and promoting fair trade.

What types of farming does Rootchain support?

RootChain supports various types of farming, including crop farming, organic farming, horticulture, and sustainable agriculture. It also provides insights into modern techniques like hydroponics, aquaponics, and precision farming, helping farmers adopt methods that best suit their land and resources.

Who can benefit from Rootchain?

Farmers, buyers, and agricultural enthusiasts can benefit from RootChain. Farmers get fair prices and direct market access, buyers receive fresh produce without middlemen, and learners gain insights into modern farming techniques and efficient farm management.

How can I sell my crops on RootChain?

You can register as a farmer on RootChain, list your crops, and connect directly with interested buyers.
The platform ensures fair pricing and a smooth transaction process.

How does RootChain ensure the quality of the produce?

RootChain encourages farmers to maintain high standards by providing farming tips and resources on best practices. The platform also allows buyers to rate the quality of products, helping maintain trust.

How can I improve my farm management using RootChain?

RootChain offers a variety of educational materials, including tips on irrigation, pest management, crop rotation, and sustainable farming practices, helping you optimize your farm management and increase productivity.

What makes RootChain different from other agricultural platforms?

RootChain focuses on providing both a marketplace for direct selling and an educational hub for modern farming practices. It combines trade with learning, empowering farmers to enhance their knowledge and profitability.
